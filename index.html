PROTECT YOURSELF, PROTECT OTHERS

Two-way filtration reduces the number of particles shared when breathing. AirPop masks create an effective barrier between you and others with market-leading filtration for total peace of mind. 

FILTRATION | BREATHING SAFETY

AirPop makes effective barriers that block airborne threats down to a diameter of 0.3μ (one-millionth of a meter), a size that is invisible to the naked eye. AirPop’s filtration efficiency is over ninety-nine percent (99.3%). Due to advanced electrostatic filter media, AirPop products provide two-way protection defending both the wearer and others.

PARTICULATE MATTERS

Pollutants smaller than 2.5 microns (μm) in diameter are known as fine particulate matter (PM 2.5).
These are often man-made or naturally occurring representing significant health threats.
AirPop masks are designed to protect even further than PM 2.5. Our materials are independently tested to ensure bacterial filtration efficacy in excess >98% and nanoparticles down to 0.1 μ m almost 25X smaller than PM 2.5.
