WASHABILITY

Each AirPop mask, when properly cleaned and maintained, will continue performing at >98% filter efficiency for at least 40 hours of wear time.

To achieve this all masks should be cleaned with 70% alcohol/soap and water for a minimum of 20 seconds and dried naturally >24 hours.

AirPop masks can also be machine washed only on delicate setting up to 10 times at 30°C on a 45-minute cycle and in our own tests filtration continues to exceed >70% efficiency.

WEAR TIME

Our masks can be used for over 40 hours of cumulative use before disposal.
